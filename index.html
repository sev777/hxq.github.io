About Me

I am a fourth-year doctoral student at Shanxi University advised by Prof. 李 茹. During my doctoral studies, I was a visiting researcher at The University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Prof. Jeff Z. Pan.

My research focuses on knowledge editing and model editing, and I am also interested in knowledge localization and model interpretability. Additionally, I have a particular interest in frame semantic parsing, such as FrameNet and Chinese FrameNet (CFN), and have participated in the development of the CFN project.
